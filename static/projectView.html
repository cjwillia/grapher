Add Task Remove Task Connect Tasks Edit Task Save Project Show Help

"Add Task" lets you create a new task, "Remove Task" deletes a task, and "Connect Tasks" adds a connection between two tasks. "Edit Task" lets you change information about a particular task and drag it around. "Save Project" saves the project (so you can access it later), and "Show Help" makes this message display.


Each project has a start date, which is the time that the project was created. When you put down a task, its offset from the left side of the screen determines its (default) deadline. For example, the middle of the screen might be due in 1 day, and a few inches to the right of the middle would be due in 1 day and 6 hours. You can the time/distance ratio by clicking on the "1 hour" at the bottom right of the screen.


Keyboard shortcuts: [B] - 'Add Task' | [C] - 'Add Connection' | [D] - 'Delete Node' | [F] - Remove all Connections from Task
